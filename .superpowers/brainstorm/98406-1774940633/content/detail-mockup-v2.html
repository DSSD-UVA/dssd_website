Renewable Energy Mapping

Python GIS Tableau
PROJECT IMAGE (optional)
OVERVIEW

Developing a geospatial dashboard to identify optimal locations for micro-grid solar installations across Virginia's rural counties. This project partners with the Virginia Department of Energy to deliver actionable infrastructure recommendations.

METHODOLOGY

We aggregated satellite imagery, county energy consumption records, and topographical data to build a composite suitability score for each census tract. A gradient boosting model was trained on known solar farm locations to validate the scoring system.

Visualizations were built in Tableau and embedded in a public-facing dashboard accessible to county planners.
